Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Viba (“the App”), by Axora Labs, values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it, in compliance with GDPR and other applicable laws.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Data: Email, Google account (for authentication).
  • Usage Data: Conversations or screenshots submitted for analysis.
  • Technical Data: Device information (e.g., OS version) for app functionality.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate AI-based insights on your conversations.
  • To save past analyses in your account (if you use the history feature).
  • To improve our algorithms (aggregated, anonymized data only).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Retention

Your submitted data (texts/screenshots) is processed securely and only stored as long as needed to deliver results or save history (if you enable it). You can delete your history at any time.

5. Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your data. We only share limited information with trusted service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, authentication) necessary to run the App.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are an EU/EEA resident, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data. Contact us at support@axoralabs.com for requests.

7. Security

We use encryption and secure servers to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

Viba is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Significant changes will be communicated via the App.

10. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@axoralabs.com.
