About Axora Labs

We build AI that helps people understand each other – starting with relationships.

Our Mission

Axora Labs is a forward-thinking AI company based in Greece, dedicated to creating tools that bridge the gap between technology and human connection. We believe emotional intelligence should be accessible to everyone – and that AI can empower people to better understand emotions, relationships, and themselves.

Meet Viba

Our flagship app, Viba, is your AI-powered relationship assistant. Whether it’s understanding emotional dynamics, analyzing text conversations, or receiving therapist-like advice, Viba helps you navigate your personal connections with clarity.
